Origin Stories

Every bean carries the voice of its micro-climate. Travel with us to the high-altitude terrains where our exceptional crops are born.

ESTATE: SANTA BARBARA

The Volcanic Ridge

  • location_on Antioquia, Colombia
  • height 1,800 Meters
  • payments Direct Circular Trade
  • cookie Chocolate & Velvet

Perched along the sheer canyon ridges of Antioquia, the Santa Barbara Estate enjoys unique micro-climates created by cold winds blowing off the Andes river systems colliding with hot tropical valleys.

This severe weather oscillation stalls cherry ripening, resulting in dense seeds overflowing with concentrated sucrose. The coffee boasts a remarkably round dark chocolate baseline and toasted sweet almond top notes.

REGION: GIDEO ZONE

The Forest Wilds

  • location_on Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia
  • height 2,100 Meters
  • spa Forest Garden Grown
  • local_cafe Cherry, Cedar & Smoke

Deep inside the Yirgacheffe forest canopy, coffee grows naturally in forest shade, integrated seamlessly with the indigenous ecosystem. Families harvest wild crops from garden trees that have stood for generations.

Roasted slowly at intense, surgical temperatures in our custom drums, these wild beans release a deeply smoky profile enriched by striking black cherry acidity and complex cedar tones.
